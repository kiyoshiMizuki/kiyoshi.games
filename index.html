Welcome to my Website!

Hi! My name is Cameron. Online, people refer to me as Kiyoshi (or Kiyo for short). I'm a 19-year-old game developer and university student with a passion for making retro-style games. I've done projects in GameMaker Studio, Unity, PICO-8, and Java. I also have some experience with sprite design and web development.

I love to collect vintage videogames, consoles, and computers. Sometimes, I try to refurbish or mod my gaming systems with the intent of restoring their value, whether that's through cleaning them, softmodding them, or installing modchips onto them.

In addition, I adore speedrunning and being a part of the speedrunning community. Occasionally, I'll find a game that I want to complete as fast as I can. However, if there's anything that I love most, it's helping out with events and commentating matches. I'm hoping that speedrunning will grow to become a popular esport.
